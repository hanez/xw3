IRC

We use the Libera.Chat IRC network for realtime collaboration.

Just click on the channel name to open a web based client in your browser for instant chat.

  • #xw3 for discussion about everything.
  • #xw3-dev for developer discussion about xw3.
  • #xw3-usr for user discussion about xw3.

Mailinglists

Mailinglists are provided by the lists.xw3.org mailinglist server.

Just click on "Subscribe" to join a list while sending an empty mail.

  • xw3@lists.xw3.org (discussion about everything) [Subscribe] [Unsubscribe]
  • xw3-developers@lists.xw3.org (developer discussion about xw3) [Subscribe] [Unsubscribe]
  • xw3-users@lists.xw3.org (user discussion about xw3) [Subscribe] [Unsubscribe]

You can also write to a list without a subscription, but your messages will then be moderated and there will be undefined delays in delivery. And you will not receive any replies if other users only reply to the list without cc'ing you.
